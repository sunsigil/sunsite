ABOUT

I am a software developer specializing in computer graphics. I recently received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Interdisciplinary Design. Currently, I am working as a gameplay and engine programmer in Minneapolis. I also do quite a bit of tools development. When I was a student, I was an officer for my university's graphics club and for its game development club. I also contributed to multiple research projects as a game and simulation developer. My primary interest within computing is graphics programming. I aim to apply my skills to develop real-time rendering features for game engines. My design speciality is typography. I have professional and educational experience both applying and creating typefaces.

My big-picture goals are:

  • to contribute to the development of games that ignite and fuel players' passion for living
  • to do design work that improves lives practically and aesthetically
